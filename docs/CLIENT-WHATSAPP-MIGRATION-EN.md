# WhatsApp Number Migration Guide for Lumino AI Bot

## What will happen?

We're connecting your business phone number to an AI bot that will respond to your customers 24/7 on WhatsApp.

**Important to understand before we start:**
- The bot will answer all WhatsApp messages sent to this number
- You won't be able to use the WhatsApp Business app on this number anymore
- You can view all conversations and manage the bot from your dashboard
- The number stays yours — customers keep messaging the same number
- Phone calls and SMS continue to work normally

---

## Step 1: Backup your chats (optional)

If you want to save your chat history:

1. Open **WhatsApp Business** on your phone
2. Tap **Settings** → **Chats**
3. Tap **Chat Backup**
4. Tap **Back Up Now**
5. Wait for the backup to complete

> [Place for screenshot: Settings → Chats → Chat Backup]

---

## Step 2: Delete your WhatsApp Business account

**This is the most important step.** Without this, we cannot connect the number to the bot.

1. Open **WhatsApp Business** on your phone
2. Tap **Settings**
3. Tap **Account**
4. Tap **Delete My Account**
5. Enter your phone number (with country code, e.g. +972)
6. Tap **Delete My Account** again to confirm

> [Place for screenshot: Settings → Account → Delete My Account]

> [Place for screenshot: Confirmation screen with phone number field]

**Note:** After deletion, if you open WhatsApp Business you'll see a registration screen. **Do NOT re-register!**

---

## Step 3: Wait 5 minutes

After deleting the account, wait **5 minutes** before notifying us. This gives WhatsApp time to release the number.

---

## Step 4: Send us confirmation

Send us a message (from another phone, by SMS, or email):

**"I deleted my WhatsApp Business account on number [your number]. You can proceed."**

---

## Step 5: Verification code

Within a few minutes, we'll send you a **verification code** via SMS to your phone number.

1. Receive the SMS with the code (6 digits)
2. Send us the code

> Example: "The code I received: 847291"

**That's it!** We finish the setup on our end.

---

## Step 6: Your bot is live!

Within a few minutes, the bot will be active. We'll send you:

- **Dashboard link** — where you can view all conversations
- **Email and password** — to log into your dashboard
- **User guide** — explaining all the tools available to you

---

## FAQ

**Q: Will I lose my phone number?**
A: No. The number stays yours. Customers keep messaging the same number.

**Q: Can I go back to regular WhatsApp?**
A: Yes, at any time. We can disconnect the bot and you can reinstall WhatsApp Business.

**Q: What happens with phone calls and SMS?**
A: Nothing. Only WhatsApp is affected. Phone calls and SMS continue working normally.

**Q: Will customers know it's a bot?**
A: The bot responds naturally and professionally. If a customer asks directly, the bot acknowledges it's AI.

**Q: What if a customer is angry and wants to talk to a human?**
A: The bot automatically detects upset customers and flags the conversation as an "escalation." You'll get a notification in your dashboard.

**Q: How long does this process take?**
A: About 10 minutes from the moment you delete the account.
